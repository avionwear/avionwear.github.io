COVID-19 Information

Dear Customers, followers, and supporters :

The world is in the middle of a crisis. We ache for those affected and pray that we all recover from this as quickly and with as little harm as possible. We believe that all of us have a responsibility and a part to play in preventing the spread of COVID-19.

As far as our operations are concerned, our design team is currently working from home. Our e-commerce store is functional, however, delays may occur. We have started shipping across India, except to Containment Zones. International Shipments have resumed as well, but take longer than usual. 

At our workspace, we're taking the following measures to make sure of the safety of our customers and employees :

- Strict social distancing protocol.

- Employees regularly using sanitizers and hand wash, as well as disinfect all surfaces. 

- Continuous use of masks and gloves in the workspace. 

Needless to say, we need your support now more than ever. Even if delayed, we will ensure that your orders get to you. Your orders will help us get through this rough patch, and help us make sure our artisan's taken care of to the best possible.
